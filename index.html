About Me

I’m Harsha - a freelance, self-taught photographer who learned through moments, not manuals. Every frame I capture is shaped by real experiences, real stories, and the quiet art of observing people as they truly are. From traditional events and engagements to intimate portraits, my goal is to create visuals that feel warm, expressive, and timeless.

If you’re planning an event and want your story captured with emotion, authenticity, and cinematic depth, feel free to reach out - let’s create something unforgettable together.
